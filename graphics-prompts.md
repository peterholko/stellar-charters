# Stellar Charters — Graphics Generation Prompts

Image-generation prompts for the game's visual assets, derived from
`game-design-doc.md` (v2.2). Each prompt is self-contained and engine-agnostic
(works with Midjourney, DALL·E, Stable Diffusion, etc.).

## How to use

1. Pick a prompt below.
2. Append the **Shared Style Suffix** so every asset stays visually consistent.
3. Use the suggested aspect ratio. For icons, ship sprites, and emblems,
   request a **transparent (alpha) background**.
4. Use the **Negative Prompt** line to suppress common artifacts.

### Shared Style Suffix (append to every prompt)

> Grounded near-future corporate science fiction; "wormhole gold rush"
> frontier aesthetic; utilitarian industrial hardware with weathered panels,
> rivets, and corporate decals; cold deep-space palette (slate blues, gunmetal,
> ice white) accented with warm corporate signal colors (amber, signal orange,
> teal); clean readable silhouettes suitable for a strategy game; cohesive
> lighting; high detail; concept-art quality; consistent line weight.

### Negative Prompt (reuse for all)

> blurry, low-res, watermark, signature, text artifacts, jpeg artifacts,
> cluttered composition, inconsistent perspective, cartoonish proportions
> (unless requested), lens flare overload, extra limbs, warped geometry.

## A. Branding & Key Art

### Game Logo / Wordmark
Logo lockup for the title "STELLAR CHARTERS". Clean, authoritative corporate
sci-fi wordmark; the word "STELLAR" in solid uppercase, "CHARTERS" lighter or
italicized to echo a legal charter document; subtle wormhole ring or warp-lane
motif integrated into a letterform; embossed metallic-on-dark treatment.
*Aspect: 16:9 and a square variant · transparent background.*

### Title / Key Art Splash
Epic establishing key art: a stable wormhole gate glowing at the edge of a
virgin galaxy, ringed by a neutral orbital station (the Wormhole Hub); private
corporate freighter convoys and escort ships streaming out along luminous warp
routes toward distant uncharted star systems; sense of a commercial land rush,
ambition and risk. Cinematic wide shot, dramatic rim lighting from the gate.
*Aspect: 16:9 · full bleed.*

### Corporation / Faction Emblem Set
A set of generic corporate charter emblems players can adopt: bold geometric
heraldry blending a trade/maritime charter feel with a spacefaring motif
(stylized warp ring, comet, star, hexagon, anchor-as-rocket); flat vector,
two-color, suitable for ship decals and stock tickers; varied shapes so each
reads distinctly at small size.
*Aspect: 1:1 grid of 6 · transparent background.*

### Wormhole Authority Seal
Official seal/insignia of the neutral "Wormhole Authority" — the Earthside
institution that regulates the gate and runs the Galactic Exchange. Formal,
governmental, circular seal: a wormhole gate centered, encircled by a starfield
border and the words "WORMHOLE AUTHORITY"; embossed metal-and-enamel look,
trustworthy and bureaucratic.
*Aspect: 1:1 · transparent background.*

## B. Ships & Convoys

Render ships in a consistent 3/4 top-down hero angle on a neutral background so
they can double as map tokens and roster art.

### Survey Skiff (Range 1)
A small, agile survey skiff: lightly armored exploration craft bristling with
sensor dishes and scanning arrays; cheap, civilian, short-ranged; minimal
weaponry; clearly a scout that charts warp routes.
*Aspect: 1:1 · transparent background.*

### Cargo Freighter (Range 1–2)
A workhorse cargo freighter / extended freighter: blocky modular hull with
detachable container pods, exposed cargo spine, big slow engines; clearly built
for hauling bulk commodities along warp routes; unarmed and vulnerable.
*Aspect: 3:2 · transparent background.*

### Escort Cutter / Frontier Escort
A lean escort cutter: nimble patrol/defense ship with point-defense turrets and
sensor pods; protective, lawful corporate security craft that guards convoys and
patrols warp tunnels; sleeker and more aggressive than the freighter.
*Aspect: 3:2 · transparent background.*

### Corsair / Raider Corvette (Privateer)
A deniable privateer corsair / raider corvette: rakish, predatory silhouette;
mismatched scavenged armor, hidden weapon hardpoints, boarding gear; menacing
but lightly built for ambush and cargo theft on frontier tunnels; no corporate
markings (deniable).
*Aspect: 3:2 · transparent background.*

### Deep-Range Clipper (Range 3)
A deep-range clipper: long, streamlined long-haul vessel with oversized drives
and extended fuel tanks; built to reach rare frontier systems and exposed deep
routes; elegant and fast.
*Aspect: 3:2 · transparent background.*

### Corporate Fleet Ship (Range 4+)
An imposing corporate fleet capital ship / heavy logistics & route-stabilizer
vessel: large armored hull, prominent corporate insignia, heavy weapon batteries
and tunnel-stabilizer arrays; projects power across clusters, supports blockades
and acquisitions; flagship presence.
*Aspect: 16:9 · transparent background.*

### Convoy Group (on a warp route)
A convoy as seen on the strategy map: a cluster of two or three cargo freighters
flanked by one escort cutter, moving together along a glowing warp tunnel lane;
faint motion trails; readable as a single grouped token.
*Aspect: 16:9 · transparent background.*

## C. Map & Environments

### Star System Map Node
A star system map node for a strategy map: a central star with a tidy iconified
orbit ring, clean and legible at small scale; provide two states — an
**unclaimed/neutral** version (muted, grey-blue) and a **claimed** version
(highlighted with a corporate accent ring and a small charter banner).
*Aspect: 1:1 · transparent background · two variants.*

### Warp Route / Warp Tunnel Lane
A charted warp route: a luminous quasi-permanent tunnel of energy stretching
between two star systems, like a navigable lane carved through space; provide
visual variants for route quality — **stable** (smooth, steady glow) and
**unstable/high-exposure** (flickering, jagged, frayed edges) to signal raid
risk.
*Aspect: 16:9 · transparent background · stable + unstable variants.*

### The Wormhole Hub
The neutral Wormhole Hub: a colossal stable wormhole gate paired with a large
neutral orbital trade station and a clearly marked "secured zone" (protected
tunnel mouth); busy with traffic but orderly and policed by the Authority; the
economic and legal center of the galaxy.
*Aspect: 16:9.*

### Colony Stage Set (Population)
A progression set of one settled world shown at five population stages, left to
right: **Outpost** (a few prefab domes), **Settlement** (a small clustered
town), **Colony** (sprawling habitats + early infrastructure), **City** (dense
illuminated urban sprawl), **Metropolis** (planet-spanning megacity, glowing
night side). Consistent angle and planet so the growth reads clearly.
*Aspect: 16:9 · five labeled variants.*

### Garden World
A lush garden world: rare, blue-green, cloud-wrapped life-bearing planet, an
oasis among barren frontier systems; visibly fertile and valuable; a coveted
food-producing prize.
*Aspect: 1:1.*

### Mining / Extraction System
A harsh extraction system: barren resource-rich planet or moon studded with
mining rigs, refineries, and ore depots, ringed by an asteroid belt being
worked by drones; industrial, dusty, utilitarian. Suitable for Ice, Metals,
Helium-3, or Rare Isotope sites.
*Aspect: 16:9.*

### Trade Depot / Route Infrastructure
A Trade Depot station: a logistics hub built in a claimed system — docking
spars, cargo cranes, fuel tanks, and patrol berths; clearly endpoint
infrastructure that secures and speeds nearby warp routes; functional and
fortified.
*Aspect: 16:9 · transparent background.*

## D. UI & Icons

Render UI icons as a matched set: same line weight, grid, and lighting, on a
transparent background, readable at 32–64px.

### Resource Icons
A cohesive set of six commodity icons in one consistent style:
**Ice/Water** (a crystalline ice shard / water droplet),
**Metals** (a stacked ingot / ore chunk),
**Helium-3** (a glowing reactor fuel canister),
**Rare Isotopes** (a radiant sealed isotope vial),
**Food** (a hydroponic leaf / ration pod),
**Credits** (a sleek corporate currency chip/coin).
Bold, flat-with-subtle-depth, instantly distinguishable at small size.
*Aspect: 1:1 each, delivered as a 3x2 sheet · transparent background.*

### Action Icons
A matched set of strategy-action icons:
**Interdict Route** (crossed/blocked tunnel),
**Patrol Route** (a shield over a lane),
**Escort Convoy** (an escort flanking a freighter),
**Survey** (a sensor sweep / radar arc),
**Claim System** (a charter stamp/flag over a star).
Single-color glyph style suitable for buttons; clear silhouettes.
*Aspect: 1:1 each, delivered as a sheet · transparent background.*

### Status / Alert Icons
A set of small status indicators:
**Raid Risk** (warning crosshair),
**Distress** (a system in alarm/red pulse),
**Unrest** (an agitated population marker),
**Charter Lapse** (a stamped document with a broken seal).
Compact, high-contrast, color-coded.
*Aspect: 1:1 each · transparent background.*

### Galactic Exchange — Market Panel
A clean game UI mockup of the Galactic Exchange screen: a single global
commodity market panel showing a list of commodities with price, trend
sparklines, buy/sell controls, and order types (Market / Limit / Standing);
dark corporate-terminal theme with amber/teal accents; data-dense but tidy.
*Aspect: 16:9.*

### Convoy Card
A game UI mockup of a "Convoy Card" panel reflecting the design doc's fields:
cargo, warp path, current route, ETA, expected payout, raid risk, security, and
status; a compact info card with a small ship token; dark UI theme matching the
Exchange.
*Aspect: 3:4.*

### Warp Route Panel
A game UI mockup of a "Warp Route" inspection panel: route name, recent traffic
history, typical cargo estimate, transit time, stability, exposure, Authority
presence, and action buttons [Interdict Route] [Patrol Route] [Escort Convoy];
dark UI theme, consistent with the Exchange and Convoy Card.
*Aspect: 3:4.*

## F. Colony Building Icons (Section 24/27)

The seven structures the player raises on a planet's colony screen. One cohesive
icon set; drop the exported PNGs into `web/public/assets/` as `building-factory.png`,
`building-reactor.png`, `building-agridome.png`, `building-miningrig.png`,
`building-habitat.png`, `building-powergrid.png`, `building-lab.png` (each 1:1, transparent).

### Colony Building Icon Set
A set of seven matching colony-building icons for a near-future corporate-sci-fi 4X
game, presented as a clean **contact sheet on a transparent background**, each
icon isolated in its own cell at identical scale, identical 3/4 isometric camera,
identical key light from the upper-left, and a shared cold-metal palette with one
warm signal accent. Each is a small, readable hero structure planted on a circular
slab of planetary ground, like a build-menu thumbnail:
1. **Factory** — a boxy automated refinery/processor with intake hoppers, conveyor
   arms and an exhaust stack venting faint vapor; amber signal lights.
2. **Reactor** — a squat fusion reactor: a glowing teal containment core inside a
   ribbed alloy shell with coolant fins and warning chevrons.
3. **Agri-dome** — a transparent geodesic greenhouse dome over green crop terraces,
   soft internal grow-lights, condensation on the glass.
4. **Mining rig** — a fortified extraction derrick over a mineshaft: a drilling
   tower, ore conveyor, and armored revetments (it doubles as defense).
5. **Habitat** — a clustered residential arcology: stacked living modules with lit
   windows, a small park ring, antennae.
6. **Power grid** — a substation: capacitor banks, transformer pylons, and humming
   conduits feeding a small distribution mast.
7. **Research lab** — a sealed laboratory module: a domed cleanroom with glowing
   instrument bays, a sensor/antenna array, and a faint holographic readout above it.
Utilitarian industrial hardware with weathered panels, rivets and corporate decals;
crisp readable silhouettes; subtle ground shadow under each; no text labels on the
art. *Aspect: each icon 1:1 · transparent background · consistent set.*

> Append the **Shared Style Suffix** and the **Negative Prompt** above. If your tool
> generates one image per prompt, reuse this exact spec for each building individually
> (same camera, light, palette, slab) so the six stay a matched set.

### Research Division Icons (set of 6)
A cohesive set of six **circular emblem icons** for the research "Divisions" of a
near-future corporate-sci-fi 4X game, on a **transparent 2×3 contact sheet**, each
in its own cell at identical scale, identical front-facing flat-emblem treatment, a
shared gunmetal-and-ice palette with one warm signal accent, and a thin etched ring
border — like crisp corporate department insignia / HUD glyphs:
1. **Prospectus** (extraction sciences) — a deposit core / ore-cross-section with a drill bit.
2. **Fabrication** (industrial engineering) — interlocking gears over a factory hopper.
3. **Navigation** (warp dynamics) — a warp-lane spiral / wormhole ring with a course vector.
4. **Colonial** (xenobiology & statecraft) — a domed habitat over a leaf / world.
5. **Security** (naval doctrine) — a hull-plate shield crossed with a cannon barrel.
6. **Acquisitions** (market intelligence) — an upward market arrow fused with an eye / spy lens.
Bold readable silhouettes, minimal detail, consistent line weight; no text.
*Aspect: each 1:1 · transparent background · matched set.*

### Secret-Project Emblems (set of 6)
A set of six **prestigious, ornate "secret project" medallions** — rarer and more
elaborate than the division icons (they're galaxy-unique endgame wonders) — on a
**transparent 2×3 contact sheet**, each a glowing circular medallion with a faint
energy aura and a fine engraved border, same cold palette with a stronger luminous
accent unique to each:
1. **Antimatter Containment** — a magnetically-suspended antimatter spark in a torus (violet glow).
2. **Nanofabrication** — a self-replicating nanite lattice / fractal foundry (teal glow).
3. **Wormhole Engineering** — an artificial wormhole gate bending starlight (cyan glow).
4. **Arcology** — a colossal ecumenopolis spire-city under a dome (warm gold glow).
5. **Orbital Dominance** — an orbital battle-station raining bombardment beams on a world (red glow).
6. **Insider Networks** — an all-seeing market eye over a web of data conduits (amber glow).
Concept-art quality, cohesive lighting, crisp silhouettes; no text.
*Aspect: each 1:1 · transparent background · matched set.*

> Append the **Shared Style Suffix** and **Negative Prompt** above to each. If your
> tool emits one image per prompt, generate each icon/emblem individually with the
> same framing, palette, and ring treatment so each set stays uniform.
